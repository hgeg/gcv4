History of Colen-Mar Volume I: Chronology
            By Heralos Syrian, 70 Melian 2E356

                            ~

    This document contains the all events is and will be occurred in Mainland of Colen-Mar in the past and future of the date this document is written. These events are planned and documented with utmost care and ensured that they will occur in following chronology.

    (?-0) Titans ruled the Colen-Mar.
    (0-1200) Dark ages. It is said that dragons ruled after titans in this time.
    (1200-1250) Exile of the Mythologos. Dragons left the Colen-Mar
    (1250-1430) Early Humanoid tribes are formed after the dragons and other mythical creatures left.
    (1430-1859) First indication of primitive human kingdoms.
    (1860-2711) Age of Thousand Kingdoms. States are loosing their political stability. Wars between kingdoms.
    (2711-2920) War of Destruction. Collapse of all political, cultural, scientific and technological structure.
    (2920-3015) Gray Age. It can be considered as second dark/tribal age of mainland.
    (3015-3250) Empire of Chaora. First occurance of great and long-living empire.
    (3250-3360) First Chaoran War is ocurred. Empire were on an edge of falling
    (3360-4724) Second Empire. The most glorious age of chaorian cult as in technological and cultural aspects.
    (4724-4761) Blood age. Empire is about to collapse. Grand Crisis.
    (4761-5119) Third Empire. High Elves of southern lands aided the empire to resurrect itself. I ended up with "Elven Heresy", which they tried to take over the empire.
    (5119-5224) Empire is collapsed into hundereds of kingdoms.
    (5224-5451) After the Reign of Chaorian Empire, political stability is reached its peak. There is not any war ocurred in this ace according to the records.
    (5451-5677) Age of Wisdom. Scientific and Philosophical boast with an unnatural speed.
    (5677-5912) The Golden Age. The climax of all cultures and races in Colen-Mar. All the people on the mainland formed the Golden Empire.
    (5912-6320) The New Age. Contuniation of Golden age. There is no record for a single incident of death throughout this age.
    (6320-7017) War of the homeland. A mysterious and superior race appeared with non-peaceful purposes. The Golden Race of Colen-Mar fought with this race.
    (7017-8971) Golden Empire has lost the battle. The left the planet with their spaceships
    (8971-8989) The Space Age. Empire grows in the space stations wandering in void.
    (8989-9117) Second boast in scientific discoveries.
    (9117-9371) Two new races found in other planets. Empire has established an alliance with these races
    (9371-9716) Mysterious race strikes again. Almost all of the alliance is annihilated. They enslaved the rest.
    (9716-9917) Golden Alliance is enslaved and systematically killed by Maurons.
    (9917-10215) Remnants of alliance rise against the Mauron Empire. They managed to escape and started to preperations of battle.
    (10215-10335) Endwar. Golden Alliance has won the war. Reports of mythical Gods helping the alliance.
    (10335-10385) The age of Great Empire. All races formed an Empire with a mix of their cultures and myth. It ruled the universe to the end.
