The Manifest of Crimson Night Order



                     Rywer man Vandurian
                             ~

It is time to rise and shine,
brothers and sisters, it is time for freedom.
The sorveign of Dark Elves is heralded to us.
Join to your bretheren and fight with our side
against the oppression and slavery.
First time in history, The God of chances smiled upon us,
to step in to the stage of masters and kings.
It is our time to be free now.

House of Mardaun is the ministry of the order,
the halls of our devoted heros and founding fathers.
The epic of dark elves will be written in here
with the blood, once flew in the veins of great fighters.
Come, and you will have our hospitality and good will.
Come, it is freedom you will see and hear,
        it is freedom you will taste and smell.

The Empire is the great enemy, fell into weakness.
And lost the commanders, the great and merciless.
Our cages now turned into ashes, and dust
Released the dark beast fild with anger and wrath
     of the lost souls of thousands from the underground.

Join us.
And the new world, will show the free elves,
of ancient wisdom under shiny sky.
